Clothing

The school has specific policies regarding clothing that students should adhere to:

  • Students must dress appropriately and wear clothing that is suitable for a school environment.
  • Clothing that promotes violence, drugs, alcohol, or contains offensive language or images is strictly prohibited.
  • Students should avoid wearing excessively revealing or distracting clothing that may disrupt the learning environment.
  • Appropriate footwear must be worn at all times for safety reasons.

Failure to comply with the clothing policy may result in disciplinary action.
