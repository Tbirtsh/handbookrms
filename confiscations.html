Confiscations

Confiscated items are items taken from students due to violations of school policies or rules. These items may include but are not limited to:

  • Cell phones
  • Electronic devices
  • Inappropriate reading materials
  • Weapons or dangerous objects

Confiscated items will be held securely by the school and may be returned to students or their parents/guardians under specific conditions. Proper identification and a signature may be required for the release of confiscated items.

For further information or to retrieve a confiscated item, please contact the school administration.
