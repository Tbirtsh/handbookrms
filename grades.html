Grades

The grading system at our school follows the point-based system. Here are the key points to note:

  • Students must receive a minimum of 3 points in a course to pass for the year.
  • An exception is made for students who receive 2 points in the second semester with no more than 2 F's for the year.
  • The point system is as follows: A=4, B=3, C=2, D=1, and F=0.

If a student fails to meet the minimum required points, they may need to retake the course or take appropriate remedial action.
