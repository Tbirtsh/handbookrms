Transportation

Bus Transportation to School

The school provides bus transportation for all students who live farther than 1.75 miles from school. Here are some key points to note:

  • Bus schedules, routes, and behavior expectations can be obtained by contacting the CFCS bus garage.
  • Students must ride only the assigned buses and board and depart from the bus at assigned bus stops.
  • Students are not permitted to ride unassigned buses unless approved by the principal for emergencies.
  • Parents/guardians are responsible for informing the bus driver when their child will not be aboard the bus.

Self-Transportation to School

Students and their parents/guardians are responsible for any transportation to and from school not officially provided by the school. Here are some guidelines to follow:

  • Students riding bicycles should wear helmets and follow bicycle safety rules.
  • Unsafe riding habits may result in the revocation of permission to ride bicycles to school.
  • Skateboards or scooters are not allowed as transportation to school.
  • The school is not responsible for lost or stolen bicycles.
